* CNN Intel Image Classification — Report
** Dataset
- Classes (n=6): buildings, forest, glacier, mountain, sea, street
- Train samples: 11228
- Validation samples: 2806
- Test samples: 3000
** Training settings
- Image size: 150x150
- Normalization: Rescaling(1/255) to [0,1]
- Loss: SparseCategoricalCrossentropy
- Optimizer: Adam
- Metric: accuracy
** Data augmentation (Task 4)
- RandomFlip(horizontal)
- RandomRotation(0.1)
- RandomZoom(0.1)
- Random brightness (tf.image.random_brightness, max_delta=0.1)
** Regularization (Task 5)
- Dropout(0.5) after Flatten
- Dropout(0.3) after Dense(64)
- EarlyStopping(monitor=val_accuracy, patience=3, restore_best_weights=True)
